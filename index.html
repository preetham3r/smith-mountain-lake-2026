Pre trip checklist

Tonight (Sunday, 6 days out)

  • Book pontoon at Lake Haven Marina, 540 721 5800. Backup: Bridgewater Marina, Parkway Marina.
  • Share this page link in the family WhatsApp.

Wednesday

  • Message VRBO host: confirm check in time, parking for 5 cars, fire pit, BBQ propane.
  • Each family confirms attendance and any dietary needs.

Thursday

  • Build the shared grocery list. Assign who brings what.
  • Confirm life jackets for every kid. The marina has them but bring your own if you have them.
  • Pull together: speakers, board games, cooking spices, cards, towels for the dock.

Friday

  • Tank fill all cars.
  • Set the WhatsApp group, pin the live location share.
  • Share this trip page link with everyone.
  • Charge phones, power banks, kid headphones.

Packing

For the lake

  • Swimsuits, two each, kids will live in them.
  • Towels for the dock and pool, separate from bathroom towels.
  • Sunscreen, reef safe. Reapply every 2 hours on the water.
  • Bug spray. Lake bugs at dusk.
  • Hats and sunglasses. Wide brim for the kids if you have them.
  • Water shoes if anyone has tender feet on the dock.

For the cooking

  • Spices, ginger garlic paste, coriander, cumin, garam masala. Rural Kroger does not stock these.
  • Pressure cooker if biryani is on the menu.
  • Cutting boards and a sharp knife. Rentals have basics, never enough.

For the kids

  • Tablets and headphones for the drive.
  • Pool floats or noodles if you have them, the marina rents them but limited.
  • Pajamas and a hoodie, lake evenings are cool.

For the drive

  • Phone car mount, charger cable, dash cam.
  • Snacks, water bottles, paper towels, trash bag.
  • First aid kit, basic meds, motion sickness for any kid that gets queasy.

Driving rules of the road

  • Caravan order. Lead car set before departure, slowest car second from front, no one drives faster than the lead.
  • Regroup points. Buc-ee's, Natural Bridge, Kroger. Wait for stragglers before moving.
  • Communication. WhatsApp group with live location shared by all drivers.
  • Fuel. All cars top up at the Buc-ee's stop. Cheapest gas in the region.
  • Slow signal stretches. Between Lexington and Smith Mountain Lake, expect spotty cell coverage. Download offline maps before leaving.

Stress test passed

  • Memorial Day traffic. Mitigated. 8 AM Saturday departure, 10:30 AM Monday return both miss the peaks.
  • Heat. Mid 80s likely. Water, hats, sunscreen built into the day.
  • Single failure point. Pontoon booking. Two backup marinas listed.
  • Weather. Rain backup plan is D-Day Memorial or board games.
  • Dietary. 16 people, mixed eaters. Kitchen first strategy. Group cooking each evening.
  • Caravan drift. Three regroup points, live location, lead car protocol.
